About Us

Hydrogreen is a team made up of students from all faculties of the Lublin University of Technology, such as Mechanics, Electricity, Mechatronics, Environmental Engineering, and Management. All members share a passion for new technologies and the construction of vehicles whose energy source is alternative fuels such as hydrogen and electricity.

The team is divided into several sections that can focus on a specific task and part of the vehicle, so that the design is refined in every detail. The team includes the Electrical, Mechanical and Marketing departments.
